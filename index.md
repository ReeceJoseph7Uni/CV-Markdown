# EveryRandSA

EveryRandSA is a South African finance platform for practical calculators and transparent product comparison.

## What you can do

- Calculate **PAYE**, **loan repayments**, **savings growth**, **inflation-adjusted returns**, and **emergency fund targets**
- Compare **savings accounts** across major banks and digital providers
- Evaluate **net effective yield** after rates, fees, and friction costs
- Review product terms, penalty rules, and eligibility conditions in one place

## Why EveryRandSA

South African savers face complex choices: tiered rates, dynamic bonuses, notice periods, penalties, and reward-linked conditions. EveryRandSA standardizes this data so users can compare products on a like-for-like basis.

## Compliance notice

EveryRandSA provides **informational content only** and does **not** provide financial advice. Always confirm final pricing, rates, fees, and terms directly with the institution before opening or switching products.
