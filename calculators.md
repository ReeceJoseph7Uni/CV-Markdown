# Calculators

EveryRandSA calculator suite is focused on practical South African decisions.

## MVP calculators

1. **PAYE Calculator**
   - Estimate monthly and annual tax outcomes
   - Include tax brackets, rebates, and age-based considerations

2. **Loan Repayment Calculator**
   - Monthly installment estimate
   - Total repayment and total interest over term
   - Sensitivity for rate and term changes

3. **Savings Growth Calculator**
   - Monthly or lump-sum savings projection
   - Nominal vs effective growth display

4. **Inflation-Adjusted Return Calculator**
   - Convert nominal return into real return
   - Show purchasing power change over time

5. **Emergency Fund Target Calculator**
   - Estimate target amount based on monthly expenses
   - Suggested saving horizon by risk tolerance

## Calculation policy

- Assumptions are versioned by date
- Every result includes a clear assumptions block
- Historical outputs remain reproducible using historical assumptions

## Important disclaimer

Calculator outputs are estimates for planning and education only, not guaranteed outcomes or financial advice.
