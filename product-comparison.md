# Product Comparison

EveryRandSA starts with savings comparison and expands to broader personal finance categories.

## Phase 2 comparison verticals

1. Credit cards
2. Personal loans
3. Insurance products
4. Investment wrappers

## Standardized comparison fields

- Product type and institution
- Pricing structure and fees
- Eligibility requirements
- Benefit triggers and reward conditions
- Penalty and cancellation terms
- Risk and suitability notes

## Design principles

- Transparent scoring criteria
- Side-by-side product comparisons
- Separation of factual terms from editorial insights

## Advisory notice

This platform supports decision-making but does not replace licensed financial advice.
