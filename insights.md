# Insights

## 2026 South African context

- Repo rate stability with a cautious easing outlook
- Inflation remains a critical real-return consideration
- Traditional banks and digital banks competing on rates, fees, and behavior-linked rewards
- Increasing importance of net-yield comparisons over headline rates

## What matters most for users

1. Real return after inflation
2. Fees and hidden friction costs
3. Access constraints and liquidity needs
4. Penalties and lock-in risk
5. Behavioral conditions required to unlock top rates

## Practical user guidance

- Match products to time horizon and liquidity need
- Avoid chasing headline rates without fee and condition checks
- Use scenario analysis to compare short vs long holding periods
- Keep an emergency fund in accessible products before locking funds

## Risk warning

Promotional and bonus rates can expire or require strict behavior criteria.
