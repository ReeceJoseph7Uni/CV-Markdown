# FAQ

## Is EveryRandSA financial advice?

No. EveryRandSA provides informational tools and comparisons only. It is not financial, tax, or legal advice.

## How often is the data updated?

Cadence depends on category: daily, weekly, or monthly. Each output shows verification status and last-updated metadata.

## Why do two products with similar rates show different outcomes?

Net outcomes include fee and friction impacts, not only headline rates.

## How are promotional rates handled?

Promotional values are flagged and separated from long-term baseline assumptions where possible.

## Do TFSA rules matter in comparisons?

Yes. TFSA annual and lifetime limits, plus over-contribution penalties, are surfaced in applicable comparisons.

## Are penalties shown?

Yes. Early withdrawal, notice-break, and other major penalty frameworks are highlighted.

## How is my calculator data handled?

Calculator inputs should be treated with strict privacy controls and minimal retention principles.

## Why are assumptions shown on result pages?

Assumptions make results transparent, auditable, and reproducible over time.
