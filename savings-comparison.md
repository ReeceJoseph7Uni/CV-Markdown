# Savings Comparison

Compare South African savings products on a standardized framework.

## Included product categories

- Liquid savings accounts
- Notice deposit accounts
- Fixed-term deposits
- Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA) products
- Shari’ah-compliant savings/profit-share products

## Comparison methodology

### Primary rank metric: Net Effective Yield

**Net Effective Yield = Gross Yield - Account Fees - Transaction Costs - Friction Fees**

This helps users compare products beyond headline rates.

## User profile presets

- Student / low-balance saver
- Salary earner
- Retiree
- High-liquidity saver
- Faith-based preference (including Shari’ah-compliant filters)

## Scenario views

- 3-month outcome
- 12-month outcome
- 60-month outcome
- With notice period vs without notice period

## Penalty visibility

Comparisons highlight:

- Early withdrawal rules for fixed products
- Notice account break penalties
- TFSA over-contribution risks

## Disclaimer

Rates and terms can change without notice. Always confirm directly with providers.
